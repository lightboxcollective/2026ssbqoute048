What's Included, Per Market

Each commercial is delivered turn-key. Travel and lodging are bundled into the per-market price.

3 Days Principal Photography

Two days dedicated to nature, environmental, and drone coverage. Day three at the Southside Bank branch for interiors and exteriors.

Drone & Aerial Coverage

Cinematic aerials of the branch and surrounding market captured during the principal photography window.

Billboard & Ad Stills

High-resolution stills captured on the branch day — selected, retouched, and licensed for billboard, OOH, and digital placements.

2 Days Post-Production

Editorial, sound design and mix, color grade, motion graphics, and a licensed music bed. One round of revisions included.

Travel & Lodging

All travel, lodging, fuel, and on-set meals are bundled into the per-market price.

Final Delivery

Broadcast and digital masters, social cuts, and selected retouched stills delivered for each market.

Terms & Acceptance

How We Move Forward

Payment

Total invoiced in three equal installments of approximately 33% — the first on signed acceptance, the second at the midpoint of production, and the final on delivery of all four spots.

Revisions

One round of editorial, color, and audio revisions per spot is included.

Usage & Rights

Southside Bank receives full, perpetual usage rights for all delivered spots and stills across broadcast, OOH, digital, and social. Lightbox retains the right to use behind-the-scenes and select B-roll for portfolio.

Weather & Reschedules

Aerial and exterior days are weather-dependent. If a re-shoot is required due to weather, it is rescheduled within the production window at no additional cost when crew availability allows.

Branch Coordination

Southside Bank will assign a point person to help coordinate any employee-specific talent and branch access for the on-location day. Lightbox Studios will handle all release forms.

Acceptance

This quote is valid for 30 days from issue. Acceptance via signed PO, email confirmation, or counter-signed agreement triggers production scheduling and the first invoice.
